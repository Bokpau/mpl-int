# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- Coaches and professional analysts looking for deep analytical data
- Esports fans, enthusiasts, and community analysts browsing career stats
- Casual viewers looking for tournament summaries and standing updates

## Product Purpose
Provide a clean, fast, and comprehensive statistics and dashboard interface for the MLBB international tournaments (MSC and M-Series World Championship). It serves as a dedicated stats frontend, enabling users to analyze player career rollups, team histories, rosters, and hero pool statistics across different tournament editions and stages.

## Brand Personality
- Analytical, premium, and clean
- Sleek dark mode, precise typography, and subtle, high-quality details that appeal to both dedicated analysts and esports enthusiasts without being overly noisy or distracting

## Anti-references
- Cliché AI/SaaS dashboard tropes (cream/sand backgrounds, ghost cards, text gradients, tiny uppercase eyebrows on every section)
- Overly loud, neon-drenched, or noisy "gamer" layouts that distract from reading stats
- Dry, generic corporate admin panels that feel like a database table dump without any esports spirit

## Design Principles
- **Data-First Clarity**: Design layouts around the readability of statistics, ensuring tables, charts, and key metrics are scannable and easy to interpret.
- **Premium Restraint**: Use the single gold accent strategically for focal points and interactive states; keep the background, text, and containers minimal and dark to let player/team/hero information stand out.
- **Structured Rhythm**: Avoid arbitrary card grids or side-stripe borders; create clear section relationships using subtle border colors and clean typographic sizing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Adherence to standard WCAG AA contrast (≥4.5:1 for body and placeholder text)
- Keyboard navigability with clear, visible focus styles (`:focus-visible`)
- Support for `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` transitions
